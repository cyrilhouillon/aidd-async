# 04 - Finalize

Consolidate the clarified idea and flag what stays open.

## Input

The clarified idea and the open points remaining after `02-integrate`.

## Output

The comment body only, a self-contained intent-level idea plus an explicit list of open assumptions and risks; `05-post` adds the skeleton and resume block.

## Process

1. **Consolidate, self-contained.** Write the refined idea as one coherent, intent-level description built from the bullets. It must stand on its own, readable without scrolling the thread above, so the downstream spec or planning step can pick it up as-is. No solution, no plan, no code.
2. **Flag the open.** List every assumption left unanswered and every risk to confirm at design time. Never present a guess as settled.
3. **Point forward.** Close with what the idea is now ready for, planning, specifying, or building, in plain words. Name no plugin, no skill, no command.

## Test

- The output is a self-contained, intent-level idea readable without the thread, plus an explicit open-assumptions-and-risks list, contains no solution or plan, and names the next move in plain words.
